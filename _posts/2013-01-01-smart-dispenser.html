This project involved designing, building, and testing a smart capacitive beverage dispenser that dispenses a given percentage of a cup. This project was a major deliverable for the EECE 281: Electronics Laboratory II course at the University of British Columbia.

I worked in a team of 6 to complete this project. Throughout the course of this project I:

  • Implemented the 68k microcontroller firmware in Assembly, enabling fluid level detection
  • Designed and machined the chassis, achieving most compact design of all project teams

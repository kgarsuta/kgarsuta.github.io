Nicknamed "TeleSpider", this robot is a hexapod designed to aid in ground disaster (e.g. earthquake, fire, etc.) search and rescue operations. The robot is controlled remotely by a user using a VR headset and a controller.

As Robotics Prototype Engineer with Archiact, I worked in a team of 5 to develop TeleSpider. Over the course of the project I:

  • Redesigned the main circuit board using DipTrace, minimizing board footprint by 50%
  • Improved the main circuit design, eliminating prior issues while adding new functionality
  • Redesigned wireless vision system, improving transmission range by 3,000%
  • Weatherproofed electrical hardware, achieving steady operation in disaster conditions

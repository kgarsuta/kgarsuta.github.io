iSeahorse is the first-ever citizen science website and app for seahorse conservation. iSeahorse harnesses the power of 'citizen scientists' - anyone, anywhere in the world who sees a seahorse in the wild - to improve understanding of these animals and protect them from overfishing and other threats.

I lead the development of this project at the University of British Columbia. Throughout the course of this project I:

  • Designed and built the entire solution from the ground up.
  • Utilized iNaturalist REST API to store and fetch research data.
